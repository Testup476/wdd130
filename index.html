Democratic republic of Congo

The Democratic Republic of the Congo (DRC) is a large central African country rich in minerals like cobalt and copper. Its capital is Kinshasa, and French is the official language. The DRC has a diverse population, vast rainforests, and the Congo River, but it faces challenges like political instability and regional conflicts. Despite this, it is culturally vibrant and has great development potential.
