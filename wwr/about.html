History

White water rafting has its origins in the early 19th century, initially serving as a means of transportation and exploration in America. The sport evolved in the 1970s when it became a recreational activity, gaining popularity with the establishment of guided tours on rivers. Today, it is a global adventure sport enjoyed by thrill-seekers on rivers with varying difficulty levels.
